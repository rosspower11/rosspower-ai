About me

Meet Ross.

I started out at Accenture — one of the largest tech consulting firms in the world — trained in design thinking and workshop facilitation. In a short time I ran well over 100 workshops and presented to more than 1,000 people.

My one thing through all of it has been making tech and business simple and accessible. I went on to lead product and engineering teams in startups, facilitate design sprints for C-suite executives, and serve as Head of Product at a NYSE-listed education company.

Today I teach founders and teams to actually build with AI — and I bring that same energy to every stage I step on.
